About

ResourceHub is an open-source project I created on September 14, 2023, with the goal of compiling helpful resources for web development initially. My aim was to provide developers with valuable resources they could use to enhance their workflow or in their journey as a developer.

Today, ResourceHub features a wide range of categories for users to choose from. Each category contains sections and resources designed to be beneficial for developers.

We're also participating in GSSoC annually to collaborate with amazing contributors and help newcomers start their open-source journey with us.
